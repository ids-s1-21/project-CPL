Brief introduction to our data set

Variables:

  • name: Athlete Name
  • sex: Athlete Sex
  • age: Athlete Age
  • height: Athlete Height in cm
  • team: Country/Team competing for
  • games: Olympic games name
  • year: Year of olympics
  • sport: Sport
  • medal: Medal (Gold, Silver, Bronze or NA)
